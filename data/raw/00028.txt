Pt c/o difficulty in breathing and severe shortness of breath. Hx of chronic fatigue, often feeling asthenic. Reports muscle weakness, esp in lower limbs, and occasional muscle cramps. Currently using a wheelchair for mobility. No hx of chest pain or cardiac issues. Poor sleep quality, often wakes up at night.
